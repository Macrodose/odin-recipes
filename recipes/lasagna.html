Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish that is made with love. The ultimate comfort food that fills the belly and feeds the soul.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced meat
  • 500ml tomato sauce
  • 200ml bechamel
  • 200g lasagna plates
  • 1 onion
  • 200g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbps olive oil

    • Steps

  • Finely dice the onion. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan and caramelise the onion for 20 minutes.
  • Add the minced meat to the pan and cook until browned.
  • Fill a large baking dish with a layer of lasagna plates, then minced meat, then tomato sauce, then bechamel, and repeat until you have two layers of everything. Top with the shredded mozzarella.
  • Cook the lasagna in an oven for 20 minutes at 200Â°C.
